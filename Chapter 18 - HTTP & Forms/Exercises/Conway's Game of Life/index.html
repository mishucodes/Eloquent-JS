Conway's Game of Life

About this Game:

This project is a humble attempt at building "Conway's Game of Life".

It's also called a "zero-player game".


The game plays itself:

Just press "Enter" / "Space", or just tap the screen.


Each cell lives or dies based on a few simple rules:

  1. Overpopulation leads to death.
  2. Isolation causes decay
  3. A delicate balance allows survival or birth
